The Time I Was Invited To The NFL Draft

Back when I was playing varsity football in high school I had the priveledge of playing since my freshman year. Over the course of the years I improved and got better to where I was not only a starter but I was also a captain of the team. My play syarted to largely effect the outcome of certain games we played.

During my time as a player and as captain, I had a close relationship with my coaches. Just recently we had a player that was graduating UCON and was a surefire to be selected in the NFL draft. Because this was not only a big moment for the player but for the school, a select amount of player were invited to be at the actual NFL draft in New York City. After long deliberation, I was chosen as one of very few that were allowed to go. It was an amazing experience that I remember to this day.
